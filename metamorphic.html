Metamorphic: The Bioactive Air Rejuvenator

Overview

Metamorphic is a groundbreaking device designed to improve air quality in urban areas where planting trees is challenging. Inspired by the natural process of photosynthesis used by algae, Metamorphic captures carbon dioxide (CO₂) from the air and converts it into life-sustaining oxygen (O₂), while also removing harmful volatile organic compounds (VOCs).

How It Works

Metamorphic employs a wind-powered microfluidic high-density photo-bioreactor. This advanced technology harnesses the power of algae-inspired artificial photosynthesis to transform CO₂ into O₂. The process involves:

  • Capturing CO₂: Metamorphic absorbs carbon dioxide from the surrounding air.
  • Transforming CO₂: Using artificial photosynthesis, it converts the absorbed CO₂ into oxygen.
  • Purifying Air: It removes harmful VOCs, improving overall air quality.

Efficiency and Scalability

Metamorphic operates without the need for external power, relying solely on wind energy. It is efficient and effective in urban environments, occupying only a fraction of the space required by full-grown trees and incurring significantly lower costs. Each unit provides the air-purifying benefits equivalent to 3-5 mature trees.

Scientific References

1. Algae and Photosynthesis: Algae play a crucial role in transforming atmospheric CO₂ into O₂ through photosynthesis. According to research, algae contribute to more than 50% of the Earth's oxygen production. (NCBI)

2. Artificial Photosynthesis: Advances in artificial photosynthesis have enabled the development of systems that mimic natural processes to convert CO₂ into O₂ efficiently. (ScienceDirect)

3. VOC Removal: The importance of removing VOCs from the air to improve indoor and outdoor air quality is well-documented. (EPA)
